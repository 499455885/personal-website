About Me

I am Wang Qi, currently a Research Scientist at the Singapore Institute of Manufacturing Technology, A*STAR. I specialize in energy storage, composites processing, and degradation analysis utilizing multi-physics simulations and machine-learning techniques. I have authored over 20 peer-reviewed papers in reputable journals and presented at international conferences. I am proficient in XCT data processing, multi-physics simulation, and co-optimization, demonstrating over 7 years of experience in successful interdisciplinary collaborative projects.
